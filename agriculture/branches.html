Branches of Agriculture

  • Pisciculture: It refers to the farming of fish for consumption and trade.
  • Sericulture: It refers to the farming of silkworms maily fed on the mulberry leaves to produce silk from their cocoons.
  • Fungiculture: It is the farming of mushrooms. It is practised in humid areas.
  • Floriculture: It refers to the cultivation of flowers.
  • Apiculture: It is the rearing of bee to harvest honey.
  • Horticulture. It is the intensive production of fruits and vegetables.
  • Viticulture: It is the cultivation of grapes for making wines.
